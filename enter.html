About the Program

The Entrepreneurship Bootcamp is designed to help aspiring entrepreneurs bring their ideas to life. Participants will learn essential business skills, connect with industry experts, and develop a roadmap to launch and grow successful startups.

Benefits

  • Gain knowledge in business planning, marketing, and financial management.
  • Access one-on-one mentorship from seasoned entrepreneurs.
  • Build a network of peers and industry professionals.
  • Receive guidance on securing funding and scaling your startup.

Program Agenda

  • Day 1: Ideation and Business Model Development.
  • Day 2: Marketing Strategies and Customer Engagement.
  • Day 3: Financial Planning and Investment Readiness.
  • Day 4: Pitch Preparation and Demo Day.
