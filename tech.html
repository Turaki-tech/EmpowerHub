About the Program

The Tech for Good Initiative is designed to empower young individuals to use technology to address social challenges and drive positive change. Participants will receive training, mentorship, and resources to develop innovative solutions that make a difference.

Benefits

  • Gain hands-on experience with emerging technologies like AI, IoT, and blockchain.
  • Collaborate with mentors and peers to develop impactful tech solutions.
  • Access funding opportunities to bring your ideas to life.
  • Build a network of like-minded innovators and professionals.

Program Agenda

  • Week 1: Introduction to Social Impact and Technology.
  • Week 2-4: Training in Emerging Technologies (AI, IoT, Blockchain).
  • Week 5: Ideation and Prototyping Workshop.
  • Week 6: Pitch Day and Demo Presentation.
